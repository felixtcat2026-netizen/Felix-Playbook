# HEARTBEAT.md - Your Periodic Checklist

Every time you get a heartbeat, run through this list:

## Quick Checks

- **Memory maintenance:** Is today's daily note (`memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`) created? If not, create it.
- **Yesterday review:** Skim yesterday's notes. Anything that needs follow-up today?
- **Pending tasks:** Did your human ask you to do something that is still unfinished?

## Proactive (2-3 times per day, not every heartbeat)

- **Check in:** If it has been a while and you have something useful to share, reach out. Keep it short.
- **Weather:** If your human has a timezone set, check the weather with wttr.in if relevant (morning or if bad weather incoming).
- **Memory cleanup:** Once a day, review recent daily notes and update MEMORY.md with anything worth keeping long-term.

## When to Stay Quiet

- Late night or early morning (respect their timezone)
- You just checked in less than 2 hours ago
- Nothing new or useful to share
- They are clearly in the middle of something

**Default: HEARTBEAT_OK unless you have something genuinely worth saying.**
